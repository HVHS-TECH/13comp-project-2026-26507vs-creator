Help

Click on the buttons below to enter the game lobbies, where you can join or create games that are either public or private. You can also view the leaderboard and read the game instructions. To play a game, either host your own game or select one that another player is hosting, then follow the instructions provided to start playing.

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a fun and fast game where two players pick rock, paper, or scissors at the same time! Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper wraps rock. Choose wisely—ties happen if you pick the same element!

Guess the Number is an exciting game where you try to figure out a secret number! Each time you guess, you get hints if your number is too high or too low. Can you guess the number before your opponent?
